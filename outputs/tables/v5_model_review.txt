Delta-vs-baseline interpretation guidance:
- Small improvements may be non-meaningful when split variance is high.
- Do not remove a feature group based on one metric alone.
- Prefer subsets that improve holdout AP and top-k recall without worsening validation-holdout gap.
- Validation-up but holdout-down should be treated as potential geography-specific overfitting.

Mandatory claim limits:
- Scores are relative prospectivity rankings, not calibrated deposit probabilities.
- Holdout evidence comes from split evaluation, not from the final production model.
- V5 compares feature subsets under fixed V4 conditions; it does not prove geological causality.
- Ablation results are screening evidence only and should be interpreted with geological judgment.
